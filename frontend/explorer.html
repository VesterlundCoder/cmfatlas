Method: Each trajectory accumulates \(P_N = K(s_0)\cdot K(s_0{+}1)\cdots K(s_0{+}N{-}1)\) (30-digit mpmath). \(K\) is \(K_x\), \(K_y\), or \(K_z\) depending on walk direction.
