Conservative Matrix Field Atlas

A public mathematical database of Conservative Matrix Fields — path-independent matrix recurrences connecting continued fractions, pFq hypergeometric series, and transcendental constants such as ζ(3), π, ln(2) and ζ(5).

BETA

The CMF Atlas is currently in its beta stage. This platform is designed to facilitate mathematical research by making Conservative Matrix Fields (CMF) more accessible to the global community. We are actively verifying existing CMFs in the database and searching for new high dimensional CMFs.
